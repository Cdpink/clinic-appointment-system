Steps

How to Book Your Appointment

Call, message, or visit us to book your appointment. Walk-ins are welcome during clinic hours.

Step 1: Click the Appointment Button

On the homepage, click the "Appointment" button to start the booking process.

Step 2: Fill Out the Appointment Form

Provide your name, contact number, preferred date, and select the doctor or service.

Step 3: Wait for Confirmation Message

Your appointment will be reviewed. You will receive a confirmation via text or call.
